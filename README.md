
# 🏦 Bank Data Analysis Dashboard 📊

This project presents an **interactive dashboard** visualizing key metrics from a bank's customer transaction dataset. The dashboard is designed in **Power BI** and aims to uncover insights such as top customers, transaction patterns, movement types, gender distribution, and more.

---

## 📌 Overview

- **Total Balance**: 💰 177.08M  
- **Total Customers**: 👥 12.04K  
- **Tool Used**: Power BI  
- **Purpose**: To analyze customer behavior and transaction trends in the banking system for improved decision-making.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

### 🔟 Top 10 Customers by Number of Transactions
![Top 10 by Transactions](Screenshot%202025-07-12%20012856.png)
- **Tonya** leads with **292 transactions**, followed by **Susan (282)** and **Tiffany (230)**.
- Useful to identify high-engagement customers for loyalty programs.

---

### 💸 Top 10 Customers by Amount Spent
![Top 10 by Amount Spent](Screenshot%202025-07-12%20012850.png)
- **Susan** is the top spender with **50K**, followed by **Tyler (42K)** and **Tim (40K)**.
- Helps in targeting premium banking offers.

---

### 🔁 Distribution by Status
![Distribution by Status](Screenshot%202025-07-12%20012843.png)
- **64.08%** transactions are **Posted**, and **35.92%** are **Authorized**.
- Indicates how much is finalized vs. still pending approval.

---

### 💳 Distribution by Movement (Credit/Debit)
![Distribution by Movement](Screenshot%202025-07-12%20012837.png)
- **Credit transactions** dominate at **92.67%**, compared to **7.33% debit**.
- Insight into customer transaction types — credit-heavy usage implies bill payments or income inflow.

---

### 👤 Count of Gender
![Count of Gender](Screenshot%202025-07-12%20012830.png)
- Slightly more **Female customers (52.19%)** than **Male (47.81%)**.
- Can help in segmenting marketing campaigns based on gender.

---

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Gender and Age Group Distribution
![Gender and Age Group Distribution](Screenshot%202025-07-12%20012823.png)
- Most customers are aged **20–30** and **30–40**.
- Balanced gender ratio in most age groups.
- Insight: Bank should focus digital campaigns on the **20–40** age bracket.

---

## 📅 Filters Used in Dashboard

- **Month Filter**: August, September, October, November
- **Day Filter**: All seven days of the week available
- This enables customized and dynamic analysis for specific periods.

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

- Target **Tonya**, **Susan**, and **Tiffany** for upselling banking products based on high transaction counts.
- Focus on **Susan**, **Tyler**, and **Tim** for premium customer services due to high spending.
- Prioritize improvements in transaction processing to move more from "Authorized" to "Posted".
- Run targeted campaigns for **20–40 age group** across both genders.
- Analyze why debit usage is so low and improve offerings (e.g., UPI cashback or debit card benefits).
